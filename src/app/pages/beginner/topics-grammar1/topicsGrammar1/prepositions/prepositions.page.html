Time
in on at
the morning Monday three o'clock
the afternoon Tuesday (morning) midday/midnight
the evening lunchtime
the summer night
Other uses of in and on:

We also use in with months and years, e.g. in December, in 2015

We also use on with dates, e.g. on 1 January
