Continents

North America


Is a large continent in the Northern and Western Hemispheres of Earth. It is to the east of the Pacific Ocean, the west of the Atlantic Ocean, the south of the Arctic Ocean, and it is the northern part of the Americas.

Europe


Is the western part of the continent of Eurasia, sometimes regarded as a continent in its own right. It is separated from Asia by the Ural Mountains in Russia and the Bosporus strait in Turkey.

Oceania


Oceania is a name used in geography for the region made up of Australia, New Zealand, New Guinea, and several other island nations in the surrounding area. Some people call this part of the world Australasia instead.
