Reported speech
Direct speech Reported speech
'I love you' He said he loved me
'I've just arrived' She said she had just arrived
'We'll come at eight' He told me they would come at eight.
  • Use reported speech to report (to tell somebody) what another person said.
  • We often introduce reported speech with said or told(+person)
  • After said or told that is optional, e.g. He said (that) he loved me.
  • Verb tense change like this:

say or tell?

You can use said or tell in reported speech but they are used differently.
You can't use said with an object or pronoun.
He said he loved me.
You must use told with an object.
He told me he loved me.
