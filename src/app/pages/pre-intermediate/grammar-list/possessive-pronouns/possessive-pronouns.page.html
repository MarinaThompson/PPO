Possessive pronouns

Whose coat is it? It's my coat. It's mine.
Whose jacket is it? It' your jacket. It's yours.
Whose phone is it? It's his phone. It's his.

  • Use possessive pronouns to talk about possession.
  • Use Whose to ask about possession.
  • Don't use possessive pronouns with a noun.
