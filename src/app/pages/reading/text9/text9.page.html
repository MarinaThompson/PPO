Having fun with family
My name is Lisa. I am a schoolgirl. I am in third grade. I have a big family: a mother, a father, a sister, a brother. We live in a large house with our grandparents. Also we have a dog and cat. I like to walk and play with my grandmother and grandfather. For this birthday my grandparents gave me a bicycle. It is very beautiful; white with little pink flowers. But I can't ride a bicycle yet. My grandma wants to teach me how to ride a bike. She has a bike too; it is big and green. Today we go to the park to ride our bike. Grandma shows me how to sit and spin the pedals. It is so easy! I love cycling with grandma!
