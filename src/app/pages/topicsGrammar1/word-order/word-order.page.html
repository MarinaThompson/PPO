Word order
Question word Auxiliary Subject Infinitive
Do you live near here?
Does your mother work?
What do you do?
Where does he live?
How do you spell your surname?
  • The word order for present simple questions with do and does is: ASI (Auxiliary, Subject, Ifinitive), e.g. Do you live here? OR
    QUASI ( Question, Auxiliary, Subject, Ifinitive), e.g. Where do you live?;
  • We often use question phases beginning with What, e.g. What colour...? What size...? What make...? What time...?
Word order in be questions:

Remember the word order in questions with be. Put be before the subject. Where are you from? What's your name? Is he Spanish?
